Our Story

A home for Greek flavors

Socrates is a family-run restaurant, created to share the recipes and traditions we grew up with.

For generations, our family has been cooking with simple, honest ingredients — extra virgin olive oil, fresh vegetables, local meats, and herbs from the Greek land.

Every plate that leaves our kitchen is made with care, following recipes passed down through time. Whether you join us for a relaxed lunch or a long dinner with friends, we want you to feel at home.
