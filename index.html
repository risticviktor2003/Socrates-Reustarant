Our Story

A legacy of authentic Greek flavors

For generations, our family has been sharing the true taste of Greece with our community. Founded with passion and traditional recipes passed down through time, Socrates brings you an authentic dining experience where every dish tells a story.

We believe in using the freshest local ingredients combined with traditional Greek cooking methods to create memorable meals that transport you straight to the Mediterranean.

At Socrates, you're not just a customer - you're part of our family. We take pride in creating warm, welcoming moments around delicious food.
